# Playbook: Ransomware Response

## Overview
Procedures to handle a suspected ransomware infection.

## Steps
1. **Isolation**: Immediately disconnect infected machines from the network.
2. **Analysis**: Identify the ransomware variant and the scope of infection.
3. **Containment**: Disable compromised accounts and block C2 communication.
4. **Recovery**: Restore from offline backups after ensuring the environment is clean.
